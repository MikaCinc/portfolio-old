// Short biography /

Mihajlo Marjanović is a student of the 4th grade of the 'Svetozar Marković' Gymnasium in Niš. He started doing web development at primary school that he completed as a student of generation and with diploma of excellence. He participated in numerous competitions organized by the Ministry of Education, Science and Technological Development, where he has more than 40 diplomas. In the gymnasium he organized workshops for programming 'Kodiak' in order to teach elementary students, and was also a member of the 'Nauči me' NGO from Nis, where he worked on similar projects. Since January 2018 he has been a FutureForward scholar where he works as a front-end web developer. Besides programming and school he likes to dance salsa, play chess and do streetworkout.
